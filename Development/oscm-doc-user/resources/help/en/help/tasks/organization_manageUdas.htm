Managing custom attributes

On the Manage custom attributes page, you can add, update, or delete custom attributes for your customers or for your customer's subscriptions.

Custom attributes are a means to store additional customer information such as data that is needed for controlling the cost and activity accounting. For example, you may want to specify the profit center to which a particular customer's revenue is to be accounted. Since there is no standard for such data, you can define your own custom attribute for storing the profit center. The custom attributes will be included in the billing data that is generated every month for services applying the native billing system of the platform. The information can then be used, for example, as a reference for an external accounting, billing, or CRM system.

When defining custom attributes for customers or for subscriptions, you can decide whether you yourself specify the attribute value for a specific customer or a specific subscription, or whether the customer enters the value when he subscribes to one of your services.

For every custom attribute, you can enter a default value. This value is used as long as no specific value for an individual customer or an individual subscription is entered. If the value of a custom attribute is to be specified by the customer, the corresponding field is preset with the default value during the subscription process. If the customer's administrator or subscription manager changes this value during the subscription process, the new value is automatically applied to all subscriptions of the customer.

To specify the value of a custom attribute for an individual customer, use the Manage customer menu option in the Customer menu. To specify the value of a custom attribute for an individual subscription, use the Manage subscription attributes menu option in the Customer menu.

Custom attributes are available for customers of your brokers when you grant them the permission to offer and sell your services. Custom attributes are not available for customers of your resellers.

To add custom attributes:

  1. To add a custom attribute for all your customers, click Add in the Define custom attributes for customers section. To add a custom attribute for all your subscriptions, click Add in the Define custom attributes for subscriptions section.
  2. On the Add custom attribute page, enter the required data. Click here for more details.
  3. To save your entries, click Save on the Add custom attribute page. You are returned to the Manage custom attributes page. The new attribute is instantly displayed.
  4. Click Add if you want to add an additional attribute. Otherwise, you can leave the page.

To edit custom attributes:

  1. Click the icon in the Edit column of the attribute to be edited.
  2. On the Edit custom attribute page, change the data by editing the fields. Click here for more details.
  3. To save your entries, click Save on the Edit custom attribute page.

    You are returned to the Manage custom attributes page. The updated definition is instantly displayed.

To delete custom attributes:

  1. Click the icon in the Edit column of the attribute to be deleted.
  2. On the Edit custom attribute page, click Delete.
  3. Confirm the deletion.

    The custom attribute is instantly deleted, and you are returned to the Manage custom attributes page.
