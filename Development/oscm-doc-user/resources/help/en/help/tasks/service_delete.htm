Deleting a service

On the Delete service page, you can delete marketable services which you do not want to offer to customers any longer.

The following prerequisites must be fulfilled for deleting a service:
  • The service must be deactivated.
  • Resale permissions for brokers and resellers must be revoked for the service.

Existing subscriptions are not affected by a deletion. Customers can continue to use them.

To delete a service:

  1. Select the service to be deleted, and click Delete.
  2. Confirm the deletion.
The service is instantly deleted.
