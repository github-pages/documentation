Copying a service

On the Copy service page, you can make a copy of an existing marketable service. Copying a marketable service may be useful for offering an existing service with slightly different properties, for example, a higher service level with a higher price, or special discounts for a promotion campaign.

Copying a marketable service means:
  • A new marketable service is created that is based on the same technical service as the copied service.
  • The service definition is copied to the new service.
  • The price model is copied to the new service.

To copy a service:

  1. From the list, select the service you want to copy.
  2. In the Service ID field, type an identifier for the service copy.

    The service ID is restricted to 40 characters and must not contain any of the following characters:

    ! " # $ % & ' * + , / : ; < = > ? \ ^ `

    The service ID is offered for selection, for example, when updating or deleting services, or when specifying upgrade and downgrade options or a price model.

  3. Click Copy.

The copy of the service is created. You can proceed with updating the service definition and the price model of the new service as required.

To provide the new service to customers for subscription, you need to define its publishing options and activate it. Optionally, you can define upgrade and downgrade options for the service and grant brokers and resellers the permission to sell it.
