Managing customers

On the Manage customer page, you can see the organization data that is currently defined for your customers. You can enter attribute values for custom attributes, provided such attributes have been defined, and you can grant discounts to customers.

To manage a customer:

  1. From the Organization ID list, select the customer whose data you want to edit.

    The list shows all customers that have subscribed to one of your services and all customers you have registered, irrespective of whether they have subscribed to a service. The organization IDs of the customers are appended to the names of the organizations.

  2. If custom attributes have been defined for your customers and your brokers' customers and you are authorized to enter the required values, specify them in the Attributes table.

    A maximum of 255 characters can be entered. The values will be included in the billing data for the customer the next time the data is generated. If no value is specified for an attribute, the attribute name will be inserted into the billing data without a value.

    The Attributes table also lists custom attributes whose values are to be entered by the customer's administrator or subscription manager during the subscription process. You cannot change the values for these attributes.

  3. To grant a discount to the customer, enter the required information in the Specify a discount for the customer section.

    A discount can be defined for a customer which applies to all subscriptions of the customer to services that use the native billing system of the platform. A discount may be valid as of the current or a future month. It can be restricted to a certain period of time. Before the time expires, the customer is notified by email so that he can react and contact the supplier.

    The discount is defined as a percentage that is subtracted from the regular total price for a subscription. It is granted for all costs of a customer that incur in a billing period in which the discount is valid. It does not matter whether the discount is valid for the whole billing period or only a part of it. If you change a discount, the new discount is valid the next time the billing data is generated. Usually, you will change a discount only in agreement with the relevant customer.

    Enter the discount information as follows:

    • In the Percent field, enter the discount value specifying how many percent will be deducted from the regular price for each of the customer's subscriptions.
    • If you want the discount to be applied as of the current month, leave the From field empty. Otherwise enter the month and year when the discounting is to be started in the format MM/YYYY, e.g. 11/2010. If you enter a month in the past, this will be ignored. Discounting always starts with the current billing period or with the month you enter.
    • If you want the discount to be applied for a specific number of months only, enter the month and year when the discounting is to be terminated in the Thru field in the format MM/YYYY, e.g. 11/2010. If you leave this field empty, the discount is valid until you specify something else.

    If you want, for example, a customer to benefit from a discount immediately for one year, leave the From field empty and enter the previous month and the next year in the Thru field.

  4. To save your entries, click Save.

The customer's administrators are sent an email informing them about the changes you have made.
