Viewing customer information

On the View customer page, you can see the organization data that is currently defined for your customers.

The following information on a customer organization is available:

  • Name of the organization
  • Email address for general communication with the organization.
  • Phone number of the organization.
  • URL of the organization's website.
  • Address of the organization.
  • Country where the organization is located.
  • Description of the organization.

To view the organization data of a customer:

From the Organization ID list, select the customer for which to view the data.

The list shows the customers that have subscribed to one of your services and the customers you have registered, irrespective of whether they have subscribed to a service.

If you are logged in as a supplier, the list also shows your brokers' customers who have subscribed to one of your services offered by a broker.

The organization IDs of the customers are appended to the names of the organizations.

The organization data is instantly displayed.
