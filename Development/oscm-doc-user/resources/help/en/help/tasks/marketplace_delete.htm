Deleting a marketplace

On the Delete marketplace page, you can remove a marketplace from your platform.

If you delete a marketplace with activated services, these services are automatically deactivated. Customers can no longer subscribe to them. Existing subscriptions, however, are not affected.

To delete a marketplace:

  1. From the Marketplace list, select the marketplace to be deleted. The list contains all marketplaces that exist on your platform.
  2. Click Delete and confirm the deletion.
