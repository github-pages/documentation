Adding payment types

On the New payment type page, you can add the payment types to be available for a supplier or reseller organization. The payment types defined here can later be made available for selection by customers.

To add a payment type:

  1. From the Payment service provider list, select the PSP that offers the payment type that you want to assign to the selected supplier or reseller organization.

    The list contains all PSPs registered and configured on your platform.

  2. In the PSP identifier field, enter the identifier or account number for the supplier or reseller as provided by the PSP. Suppliers and resellers need to have a contractual relationship with the PSP. The PSP configures the channel for a supplier or reseller and issues an identifier, usually an account number, so that payments can be immediately linked to the supplier's or reseller's account.
  3. Select the types of payment that the selected supplier or reseller may offer to his customers. Next to Payment, you see the payment types offered by the selected PSP. The available payment types depend on the configuration of the PSP.
  4. To save your entries, click Save.
    Note: You can no longer remove a payment type which has been assigned to a supplier or reseller organization.

You are returned to the page for managing the organization.
