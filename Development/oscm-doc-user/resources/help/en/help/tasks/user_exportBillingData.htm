Exporting billing data

On the Export billing data page, the following tasks can be performed:

  • Export the billing data for one or several customers for a specific time frame. This can be done by resellers or suppliers.

    You can use the exported data as the basis for creating invoices for your customers.

  • As a reseller, broker, marketplace owner, or supplier: Export the revenue share data for your organization for a specific time frame.

    As a platform operator: Export the revenue share data for all organizations with a specific role for a specific time frame.

    You can use the exported data as the basis for collecting or paying your shares, depending on your contracts with the other participating parties.

The exported data can be saved to an XML file or opened in an editor of your choice. You can edit the data and work with it as required. For example, you can forward the data to an accounting system.

Note:

For a detailed description of the elements and attributes that may occur in the XML file, refer to the Developer's Guide.

To export billing data for customers (suppliers and resellers):

  1. In the Billing data type field, select Customer billing data.

    The field lists all customer organizations that you have created or that have subscribed to at least one of your services.

    If you are logged in as a supplier, the field also lists your brokers' customers who have subscribed to one of your services.

  2. Select the check boxes of the customers whose billing data you want to export.
  3. Specify the start and end date of the time frame for which you want to export the data by clicking the From and To field and selecting the date in the calendar that is provided.
  4. Choose whether to save the data to a file (<date>BillingData.xml) or to view it instantly in an editor of your choice.

To export revenue share data for organizations :

  1. In the Billing data type field, select Revenue share data.
  2. In the Organization role field, select the organization role for which to export the revenue share data. Depending on the roles assigned to your organization, you can select Supplier, Broker, Reseller, or Marketplace owner.
  3. Specify the start and end date of the time frame for which you want to export the data by clicking the From and To field and selecting the date in the calendar that is provided.
  4. Choose whether to save the data to a file (<date>BillingData.xml) or to view it instantly in an editor of your choice.
