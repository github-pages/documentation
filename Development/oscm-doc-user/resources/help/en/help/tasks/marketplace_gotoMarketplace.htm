Going to a marketplace

From the Go to marketplace page, you can directly access a marketplace.

To go to a marketplace:

  1. From the Marketplace list, select the marketplace you want to access.

    The list contains all marketplaces to which you have been granted access by or for which you are registered as the marketplace owner.

  2. To access the selected marketplace, click Go To.

The marketplace is displayed and you are automatically logged in. Depending on your browser settings, the marketplace is displayed in your current tab, a new tab, or a new browser window.
